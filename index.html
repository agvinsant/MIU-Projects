My Little Helper

A personal chore list for that slightly forgetful person!

Add & Display Chores

This is a web based app that will run straight from your browser. Lists are saved to your browser's local storage so you can bookmark this app in your browser and use it to store your to do list.

This app is a current project in my Visual Frameworks course for Full Sail University.

I created this app because I have a bad case of A.D.D. and often forget things my wife tells me to do. Hopefully you can find it useful too.
